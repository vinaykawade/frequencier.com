The Frequencia offers an advanced spectrum management system framework comprising of radio resource management algorithms, functional blocks and information repository designed to address various issues related to wireless industry, regulators and end users. It couples leading edge insight with efficient technology development capability using cloud-based processing to deliver scalability and manageability. The basic idea in its simplest form is that if a primary user is not using his spectrum at any particular location or time, it is made available for secondary users to use on an opportunistic and dynamic basis, as illustrated by the below figure.



Key Capabilities:


  • Dynamically provide best spectrum connectivity options i.e., power, frequency, duration to all forms of wireless infrastructure/devices on national basis
  • Enforce regulator or license holder specified interference protection thresholds
  • Manage coexistence to improve utilisation efficiency of shared spectrum systems
  • Enforcement of SLA contractual agreements between various stakeholders
  • Real-time spectrum utilisation map for the country
  • Adapt to a suit different business and regulatory models through software-determined interfaces
