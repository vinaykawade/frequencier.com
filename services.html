Spectrum Inventory Service This information service monitors the spatial and temporal loading patterns and dynamics and presents a real-time spectrum utilisation map for the country. Based on the observed spectrum usage behaviour patterns, clients have a predictive and proactive spectrum management capability. The real-time spectrum utilisation information in the spectrum inventory is used to enforce primary system protection and also simplify the spectrum allocation and clearing process.


Spectrum Booking Service Frequencia's spectrum booking platform provides access to spectrum which is predictable, measurable and offers a degree of certainity. This helps service providers to provide assurances for certain services such as rural broadband or local events and make the required network investments . With automated management of spectrum resource, the booking platform enables commercial intermediaries like band managers and spectrum brokers to aggregate dynamic and volatile demand and trade spectrum.


Spectrum Management Library To manage efficient use of spectrum, Frequencia offers a rich set of algorithmic functions for spectrum resource management based on SDR functionality, Propagation modelling and using GIS, House and Road databases. The library functions are embedded in different network elements i.e., radio chipsets, base stations or access points and network management platform and is ported and supported for wide range of vendor hardware configurations.
