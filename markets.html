Regulators: Frequencia's ASMS functions as electronic regulator to issue licenses dynamically, reducing regulators and licensee's operational costs and time delays. The spectrum inventory provides a real-time monitoring of spectrum utilisation. Frequencia platform is extensible as it supports novel regulatory and commerical spectrum sharing models.


Service Providers, Network Operators: Frequencia's ASMS offers a dynamic wireless selection capability to reduce capacity and coverage provisioning costs, improves connectivity choices for operators/users, erduced times scales and an alternative to using conventional wireless networks and technologies.


Equipment Manufacturers: Our product provides significant new market opportunities with opportunistic access to unused spectrum. It exploits technology developments in SDR/CR area and current spectrum deregulation trends to enable generic processors support a wide variety of services/applications.


Intermediaries: The platform with its real-time spectrum inventory and our mathematical business analytic library, supports spectrum intermediaries like band managers and spectrum traders.
